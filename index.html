Arequipa, Peru

Arequipa is the second largest city in Peru and is located in the southern Andes. It is surrounded by three volcanoes and is known as the "White City" because many buildings are made of volcanic stone. Arequipa is famous for its colonial architecture, traditional cuisine, and breathtaking views of the Misti volcano.
